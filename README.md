# Universal Payroll Hub V6.0

Expanded from two different earnings statements to support a wider audience.

New payroll model:
- Salary, hourly, contract, and commission pay basis
- Rate and hours or units
- Regular, overtime, bonus, commission, reimbursement, and other earnings
- Federal, state, local, Medicare, Social Security, and other taxes
- Retirement, medical, dental, vision, HSA/FSA, life, and other deductions
- Employer retirement match
- Split direct-deposit tracking
- Automatic Gross Pay, Total Taxes, Total Deductions, and Net Pay
- Flexible employee ID, department, company address, and company branding
- Reports and CSV export for expanded fields
- Existing Version 5 records migrate into the new model
